How It Works

From Brief to
Final Cut

Every project follows our proven 5-step process — refined across 200+ videos and 4 years of storytelling.

01
Discovery & Brief

We dig deep into your audience, goals, and key message. A thorough brief means zero back-and-forth surprises later.

02
Script & Storyboard

Our writers craft a tight script, then illustrators sketch every scene into a visual storyboard you approve.

03
Voiceover & Music

Professional voice talent brings your script to life. We match tone, pace, and background music to your brand.

04
Animation

The magic happens here. Our animators sync every hand-drawn element to audio with fluid, watch-again-worthy motion.

05
Delivery & Support

You receive 4K exports, source files, and 30-day revision support. We're with you past launch day.
